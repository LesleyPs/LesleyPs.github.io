Wireframes
Overview Clicktripz (now Tiki) provides context-aware advertising solutions for travel websites. This case study focuses on improving publisher experience by developing "Tower," a web application that streamlines onboarding, account management, and performance tracking, and provides tools for our sales team.
Problem Statement The manual processes for publisher setup and performance insights, handled by the Product Integrations team, limit internal team efficiency, complicate publisher onboarding, and hinder access to performance data. This impacts our ability to scale partnerships with smaller travel websites, lowers publisher satisfaction, reduces potential revenue, and often prevents us from partnering with small publishers.
Goals Business Goals Increase active smaller travel publishers, improve internal team efficiency, and increase revenue. Publishers Goals Streamlined onboarding, self-service account management, easy access to performance data, effective campaign optimization, and timely support. Sales Team Goals Reduce manual tasks with self-service tools that support publisher account management and proactive support. Research The research focused on understanding the current onboarding process, data needs, technical constraints, and key metrics by interviewing the Sales and Product Integration teams. I also evaluated similar or related products such as Google Ad sense.
