The Solution A user experience that streamlines onboarding, account management, and performance tracking for our product publishers. It also provides tools for the internal sales team and helps them easily spot upsell opportunities.
Because of time constraints, little testing was done. However, I worked with the product manager and engineering team on several iterations.
Key Features Tower includes a Key Metrics Dashboard, Speedy Self Service Onboarding, Product Placement Controls, and Troubleshooting.

Problem Statement Having the Product Integrations team handle publisher onboarding and KPI metric gathering takes up a lot of their tech power, which can make it tough to partner with smaller publishers. Let's Make Partnering Easier! By creating a simple self-service way for publishers to get started and see their performance, we can free up the Product Integrations team to focus on bigger things and open the door to awesome partnerships with all kinds of publishers!
