Problem Statement
Having the Product Integrations team handle publisher onboarding and KPI metric gathering takes up a lot of their tech power, which can make it tough to partner with smaller publishers.
Provide publishers with streamlined onboarding, self-service account management, easy access to performance data, effective campaign optimization, and timely support. This will help our sales team reduce manual tasks and offer proactive support.
User Goals
The research focused on understanding the current onboarding process, data needs, technical constraints, and key metrics by interviewing the Sales and Product Integration teams.
In essence, it's a dual-purpose tool aimed at improving efficiency and effectiveness for both external partners (publishers) and internal staff (sales).
